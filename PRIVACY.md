# FocusFlow Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 21, 2026

FocusFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information. 

Our core philosophy is simple: **Your data belongs to you.**

## 1. Data Collection and Storage
FocusFlow operates entirely on your local device. We do not use external servers, and we do not transmit, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.

**What we track locally:**
- **Website URLs & Time Spent:** To provide you with analytics on how you spend your time.
- **User Activity:** To determine if you are actively browsing or if your computer is idle (so we pause the timer).
- **Settings & Preferences:** Your blocklists, focus schedules, and goals.

**Where it is stored:**
All of the data mentioned above is stored locally within your browser's storage on your own computer. We do not have access to this data.

## 2. Browser Permissions
To function properly, FocusFlow requires certain browser permissions. We only request permissions that are absolutely necessary for the core features of the extension:

- **Storage / Unlimited Storage:** To save your settings and historical time-tracking data locally.
- **Alarms / Idle:** To run focus timers efficiently and stop tracking when you step away.
- **Declarative Net Request:** To instantly block access to distracting websites based on your rules.
- **Tabs / Scripting:** To accurately track active time spent on the websites you visit.
- **Host Permissions:** To allow tracking and blocking functionality across the websites you navigate to.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not integrate with any third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics) or advertising networks.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through an update note within the extension.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how FocusFlow handles your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
